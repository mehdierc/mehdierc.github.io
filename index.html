About me

{% include image.html url="/images/mypic-1.jpg" caption="Caption" width=300 align="left" %}

I recently finished my B.Sc. degree in Isfahan University of Technology (IUT) majoring in Computer Engineering - Software. Currently, I am a research assistant at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Advanced Robotics and Mechatronics Laboratory (ARMLab), and my work is related to design and fabrication of different robotics and mechatronics systems. Furthermore, I'm in close association with University of Isfahan, Department of Mechanical Engineering.
I have a well-rounded background in robotics, specifically in computer vision, artificial intelligence, mechatronics and control theory. Moreover, I have a broad experience in programming and algorithms, design and implementation of embedded systems, digital circuits, and real-time controllers.
I'm very interested in robotics. I've started learning and experiencing electronics and robotics since childhood, and I'm planning to spend the rest of my life on doing research and working in robotic related areas.
